The October Spell 🍂

They whisper that October breathes softly—
Yet every sigh feels like a storm.
You don't just meet people this month,
You collide with souls that feel like home.

It's the season of half-lit moons,
Of coffee gone cold and hearts too warm.
Where every glance could mean forever,
Or fade before the dawn.

Maybe that's why October aches—
It's never just weather, it's memory.
A song you almost remember,
A touch that almost stays.

Because love in October isn't gentle,
It's a wildfire wearing calm skies—
And whether it heals you or breaks you,
You never leave it unchanged.

Ehsaas-e-Raaz ✨

Ab na pehle si baatein hain,
Na pehle se jazbaat,
Chehre wahi hain lekin,
Kho gaye wo ehsaas-e-raaz.

Muskaan ke peeche ab sau
Afsaane chhupe rehte hain,
Dil ke andar khud se bhi kai
Sawaal bante rehte hain.

Waqt ne badal diya har
Rang, har dastaan,
Phir bhi dil dhoondhta hai
Ek nayi subah ka armaan.

~ Shubhra 🖋️✨🌙

She Called It Hope 🌸

Deep down in the mind,
An echo of resilience.
Bouncing up and down,
Expecting a revival.

Trapped in void,
Struggling among the dilemma.
A door was opened,
She called it hope; the hope to live...

Wrapped in brood,
Juggling among the conundrum.
A door was closed,
She called it hope; the hope of peace...

Strapped in distress,
Grappling among the torment.
A page was flipped,
She called it hope; the hope of joy...

Flapped in repress,
Muffling among the stridence.
A page was reversed,
She called it hope; the hope to do over...

Down the road was her destiny,
All in form was her soul.
To receive the revived world,
In full charm and glory.

~ Shubhra ✨🖋️🌙

I Am A Woman 👑

I am a woman, not just a body,
I am the threshold of your heart.

I am a woman, not just pain,
I am a great saga.

I am a woman, not just a sting,
I am a whole clan that is contained within me.

Don't make the mistake of considering me tearful eyes,
I am bright eyes!

Don't look at me with pity,
I am the embrace of a brave man!

Don't put me in the dock of suspicion,
I am full of affection and love!

Don't tie me in the shackles of four walls,
I am the shelter of your difficult circumstances!

Don't consider me just an idol of pomp,
I am the charm of your pure heart!

I don't need any simile,
Be it Kali-Durga, sister, or mother.

Just like you have a body and mind,
I also have a body and mind.

Every breath comes and goes!

Actually, women are usually like Shiva's bow,
No man has the "manliness" that someone can break it.

But it breaks itself,
Bends,

With selfless love,
With pure affection.

~ Shubhra ♀️✨🖋️

The Art Of Hiding 🌙

After years, I have mastered the art of hiding,
The scars, the bruises, and the tears while riding.

In order to hide my weakness,
To prevent the display of mental sickness.

I really want to give up and live my way,
But the dreams of mine beg me to stay.

I have to cope up with the inhuman way of life,
But I will never allow my heart to wipe.

Sometimes, I thank destiny for the life I got,
But additional to some haunting memories
that I partially forgot.

I hate life for snatching people away,
But at the end, I still work on life's way.

~ Shubhra ✨🖋️

My Silent Truth 🦋

            They say I've never seen myself,
            the way I "should" be seen,
            the way the world dictated
            my dictionary mean;

            they said my eyes should filter,
            for me to see what's real,
            and my whole mouth be covered;
            then shall my worth be sealed;

            Maybe they better know;
            maybe I have just been blind,
            maybe they see and feel me more;
            when their beliefs abide,

            never my eyes,
            never my honest flaws,
            never my lonely truth,
            discarded by their laws;

            now my life's rolling,
            on their hills,
            who's edges scrapped,
            away my skills;

            kept rolling over down;
            my aimless quickened pace,
            somewhere my dreams have never found,
            somewhere so loud their praise,

            their screams awakened souls;
            their cheers did echo;
            on densely crowded mountains,
            I can't say hello;

            their million words against my single sigh;
            never did mean to theirs;
            only on mine.

         ~ Shubhra 🖋️✨

Let Me Stay A Bit Longer ☕

Snuggling into pillows,
Let me hide a bit longer.

Running by the shore,
Let me feel the softness longer.

Pouring my heart out,
Let me write a bit longer.

Drenching in the rain,
Smell a café at the corner.

The coffee goes in,
Let me sip a bit longer.

Small such things, which I wished,
Stayed a bit longer.
